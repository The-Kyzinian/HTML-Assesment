Magazine: The 64 Squares

The official magazine of the Henderson High School Chess Club, the 64 Squares is edited by James O'Callaghan and Ardeth Waugh, and printed by Henderson High School. Physical copies are available at HHS. Please click the download button to download a pdf copy of the issue of your choice. Currently, 1 issue of the 64 Squares have been published, both physically and digitally.
